## Quick Start

Create an episode featuring Anil Singh as your AI guide.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Ramayanam.ai">
    ````
    Visit https://ramayanam.ai and log into your account.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Go to Create > New Episode">
    ````
    Click the "+" icon and select 'New Episode' to begin.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Anil Singh as your avatar">
    ````
    Choose 'Anil Singh' from the list of digital avatars.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Write your story or prompts">
    ````
    Add a scene description or let Anil Singh guide the dialog.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish and share">
    ````
    Click 'Publish' to make your episode live for others to explore.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
