## About Anil Singh

Anil Singh is a featured digital avatar on Ramayanam.ai, designed to share insights and wisdom from the Ramayana.

## What does he do?

- Guides users through interactive Ramayana episodes
- Answers questions using AI-powered conversations
- Embodies core traditional values in his responses

## How to interact?

Just start a chat with Anil Singh on the platform and ask questions about the Ramayana story, values, or lessons.
